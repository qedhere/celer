## FAQ

> What is Celer?

Celer is a note taking app that helps you organize your thoughts and ideas.
It is designed to be simple and intuitive to use, so you can focus on what matters.

> What is the difference between Celer and other note taking apps?

Celer is meticulously handcrafted to optimize speed so you can share your work instantly. Built on Next.js 13, it is blazingly fast and has a small footprint.

> What does Celer mean?

Celer is Latin for "speed".

> How can I contribute to Celer?

Celer is open source, and you can contribute to it on [GitHub](https://github.com/qedhere/celer).
For more information, check out the [Contributing](/docs/contributing) guide.
